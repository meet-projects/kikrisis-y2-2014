About Us

A few words about the website...

Kikrisis is a website designed for potential investors looking for products designed to help others in a time of danger. Through this website, users with great ideas can connect with investors looking to find the right items to have in times of war, natural disasters or any other crisis one find themselves in. With the help of this website, everyone can be the creator and the investor, helping the world produce better crisis products.
